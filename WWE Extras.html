WWE Ruthless Aggression Era - Extras

Memorable Moments

John Cena's Debut Against Kurt Angle

June 27, 2002 - A rookie John Cena steps up to Kurt Angle's open challenge, showing "Ruthless Aggression" and nearly defeating the Olympic Gold Medalist.

Evolution Forms

Triple H, Ric Flair, Randy Orton, and Batista come together to form one of the most dominant factions in WWE history.

Eddie Guerrero Wins WWE Championship

No Way Out 2004 - Eddie Guerrero defeats Brock Lesnar to win his first WWE Championship in an emotional victory.

Did You Know?

  • The term "Ruthless Aggression" was first used by Vince McMahon in a promo to the WWE roster in 2002.
  • Batista held the World Heavyweight Championship for 282 days during his first reign.
  • John Cena's rap gimmick was created after he was caught freestyle rapping on a tour bus.
  • Rey Mysterio's 619 move is named after the area code of his hometown San Diego.
  • Goldberg originally got his start in WCW in 1997 during the Monday Night Wars between WCW and WWF(Currently WWE).
  • Sable returned to WWE in 2003 and during that time she met Brock Lesnar. She officially retired in 2006 after marrying Lesnar.
