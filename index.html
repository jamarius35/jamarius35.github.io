WWE Ruthless Aggression Era

The Ruthless Aggression Era was a pivotal time in professional wrestling history, spanning roughly from 2002 to 2008. The Attitude Era had just ended and Vince McMahon was looking for an updated more edgy experince for fans. This period was characterized by intense competition, rising stars, and groundbreaking storytelling. Below are my top 3 favorite superstars from this period.

Brock Lesnar

Batista
